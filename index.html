About Us

We’re motorcycle enthusiasts who like nothing more than the smell of burnt rubber on road. We’ve peddled the best café racers and the worst trikes imaginable, so we know how to get you in the right saddle for you to ride your ride. Founded in 2022, we know the latest models to the classics. Whether you're looking for a sturdy beast to get you to Timbuktu and back or a smart little piece to cut down on the morning commute, we've got it all. No matter what, we'll make sure you'll have something that gets the blood pulsing and keeps you safe. The freedom of the road is yours to take.

Get Out and See the World

If you're looking to hit out on the open road and find where it'll take you, trust us to set you up with something right. All you'll need is the petrol to keep the bike going and the will to keep on pressing into the world. Feel the wind in your face and keep your eyes on the road without ever needing to worry about the bike. It'll take you wherever you want to go and will leave fears of the unknown many miles behind.

Experience new cultures. See a different side of life. Feel what it means to live in this world...

Get your ride from Bikes and Trikes

Travel in Style

If you're riding local, hit the road with one of our urban bikes. It'll take you to heaven and back - and to work too, if you can even think of stopping. You'll dodge between the traffic, make the green lights, and wow passers-by.

You'll see your city like you've never seen it before. Close to the road. Close to the people. And closer to streets and avenues that you never went down before...

Make the most of where you live with Bikes and Trikes
